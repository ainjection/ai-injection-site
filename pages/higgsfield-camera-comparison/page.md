### Prompts Used:

### Girl

Extreme macro closeup portrait of beautiful young woman in her 20s. She has vibrant, thick ginger-red hair with individual fine strands and flyaways visible. Her complexion is fair with a dense, realistic pattern of light brown freckles across her cheeks and nose. Her eyes are a clear, light sea-foam green with intricate detail in the iris and natural wetness. No makeup, natural eyelashes, and soft pink natural lips with visible skin texture. Soft natural light from a side window creating a catchlight in the eyes. Raw photo, hyper-realistic skin pores and fine peach fuzz, neutral color temperature, sharp focus on the iris.

### Old Man

A cinematic close-up portrait of a gaunt 80-year-old man with a long, square face and high, sharp cheekbones. He has deep-set, piercing ice-blue eyes surrounded by heavy crow’s feet, and a prominent, slightly crooked aquiline nose. His skin is deeply tanned and leathery, with a specific small mole on his right temple. He sports a thick, wiry, white beard that is trimmed short and a receding hairline with wispy white hair swept back. He is wearing a charcoal grey heavy-knit wool sweater. He is working in a dimly lit studio. A single shaft of warm sunlight hits his face and hands, highlighting fine pores and dust motes in the air. Workshop tools in the background. Extremely detailed texture, natural color grading, high dynamic range.

### Astronaut

A realistic, wide-angle cinematic shot of an astronaut sitting alone on a grassy alien hillside at sunset. The astronaut, wearing a detailed white space suit with orange accents and a bulky life-support backpack, is seated in profile among a vibrant field of purple, yellow, and pink wildflowers. They are gazing toward a bright, golden sun hovering just above a distant mountain range. Domineering the upper left sky is a massive planet partially illuminated, hanging close to the horizon. The scene is bathed in warm, orange-gold light that highlights the petals of the flowers in the foreground and reflects off the astronaut’s helmet visor.
