# Character & Actor Catwalk

Character & Actor Catwalk[Admin@AIShotStudio](https://aishotstudio.com/author/adminaishotstudio/ "Posts by Admin@AIShotStudio")2026-04-25T19:32:50+00:00

A set of three prompts used to create complete character and actor catwalk video. More Viral video prompts: [AI School](https://aishotstudio.com/go/aischool).

[![Sorry, your browser doesn't support embedded videos.](https://aishotstudio.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/Hulk-Actors.webp)](https://aishotstudio.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/Hulk-Actors-sm.mp4)

## **Prompt #1** – Paste it into an AI chat (Gemini, ChatGPT, etc.) to get a list of actors who portrayed the character. Chat will generate a table with all actor names, movie titles & year, and iconic background description. Replace the placeholder in square brackets with the character name.

Copy to Clipboard

Syntax HighlighterProvide a complete table of all actors who have played [REPLACE WITH CHARACTER NAME] in a movie/TV show. The entries must be sorted chronologically by the movie's release year, starting with the oldest film at the top. The table must contain exactly three columns:
1. Actor Name
2. Movie Title (Year)
3. Iconic Background (Provide strictly one sentence describing the character's most visually memorable environment in that specific film without including the character themselves, eg, city ruins, underground lab, rooftop, etc. There should be enough space for the character to walk toward the camera at least 10 steps.

## **Prompt #2** – Paste it into the Grok Imagine video generator to generate a character and actor image. Fill in the character name, movie title & year, actor name, and background description. For better results, add reference images.

Copy to Clipboard

Syntax HighlighterFull-body frontal close-up shot. Two figures walking close side by side.
On the left is [CHARACTER NAME] from movie [MOVIE TITLE AND YEAR] @[REFERENCE IMAGE #1], scaled strictly to their accurate, in-universe movie proportions.
On the right is [ACTOR NAME] @[REFERENCE IMAGE #2] who plays the character, rendered at a normal, lifelike human scale.
Both are looking straight ahead with confident expressions, walking toward the camera. Both characters are fully realistic and highly detailed.
Camera: Candid high-quality digital snapshot, iPhone 15 Pro Max 24mm f/11, 8k, entirely sharp deep focus, raw and clean, zero blur, zero film grain, no filters, no text.
Background: [INSERT BACKGROUND]

## **Prompt #3** – Paste it into the Grok Imagine video generator to generate a video clip.

Copy to Clipboard

Syntax HighlighterCharacters walk confidently, with a natural tempo toward the camera, the camera follows.
