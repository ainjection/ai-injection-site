# 26 “Camera Views”

26 “Camera Views”[Admin@AIShotStudio](https://aishotstudio.com/author/adminaishotstudio/ "Posts by Admin@AIShotStudio")2026-01-26T01:27:25+00:00

## Shot Types, Camera Angles, Perspective & Lens

AllShot SizesCamera AnglesPerspective & Lens

### Extreme Wide Shot (EWS)

Extreme wide shot, establishing shot, vast landscape, tiny subject

### Wide Shot (WS)

Wide shot, full body, long shot

### Full Shot (FS)

Full shot, full body visible, head to toe

### Medium Long Shot (MLS)

Medium long shot, framed from knees up, american shot, three-quarter length

### Medium Shot (MS)

Medium shot, waist up, mid-shot

### Medium Close-Up (MCU)

Medium close-up, chest up, head and shoulders

### Close-Up (CU)

Close-up, face shot, tight framing

### Extreme Close-Up (ECU)

Extreme close-up, macro eye, ECU, detail shot

### Macro Shot

Macro photography, microscopic view, highly detailed texture, 100mm macro lens

### Eye Level

Eye level shot, neutral angle, straight on, camera at eye height

### Low Angle

Low angle, looking up, imposing view, hero angle

### High Angle

High angle, looking down, inferior angle, subject looking up

### Worm’s Eye View

Worm's eye view, ground level, looking straight up, ant's view

### Bird’s Eye View / Overhead

Bird's eye view, overhead shot, top-down view, 90 degree angle

### God’s Eye View

God's eye view, satellite view, high altitude, vertical angle

### Dutch Angle / Canted Angle

Dutch angle, canted angle, tilted horizon, diagonal shot, disorienting

### Point of View (POV)

POV, first-person perspective, seeing through eyes, hands visible

### Over-the-Shoulder (OTS)

Over-the-shoulder shot, looking past shoulder, blurry foreground shoulder, OTS

### Two-Shot

Two-shot, 2 subjects in frame, medium shot, side-by-side

### Fisheye View

Fisheye lens, barrel distortion, ultra-wide 180 degree, vignette

### Isometric View

Isometric view, orthographic projection, 3d render, diorama

### Telephoto / Compression

Telephoto lens, long focal length, compressed background, flattened perspective

### Selfie View

Selfie angle, arm's length, front facing camera, phone camera distortion

### Drone View

Drone view, aerial photography, hovering, high altitude

### CCTV / Security Cam

CCTV footage, security camera, surveillance view, grainy, high angle corner

### GoPro / Action Cam

GoPro footage, action camera, body cam, wide angle, barrel distortion
