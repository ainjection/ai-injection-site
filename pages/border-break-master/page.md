## **Master Prompt**. Paste it into an AI chat (Gemini, ChatGPT, etc.) to generate prompts.

Copy to Clipboard

Syntax HighlighterAct as an expert AI video workflow prompt generator. Based on my input text, you will provide me with two outputs: a customized Nano Banana Pro image prompt and a customized Grok animation prompt.
Idea: [INSERT YOUR IDEA HERE - e.g., A hyper-realistic digital rendering of a young blonde woman in a vibrant 1980s retrowave style, OR a handsome young man in a cyberpunk alleyway.]
Part 1: Image Prompt Generation
Based on my input text, generate a Nano Banana Pro image prompt inspired by the reference prompt below. Retain the extra-thick white border, the poster format, the luxury campaign aesthetic, the extreme wide-angle perspective, and the 3D border-break effect. However, you must adapt the model's gender, pronouns, and appearance to perfectly match my Idea, while also varying the fashion brand, the location, the object the person is holding, and the minor styling details.
Reference Prompt to use as your blueprint:
"High-fashion poster with an extra-thick clean white border (very large border), luxury campaign aesthetic, cinematic low-angle 12mm ultra-wide lens photo with extreme barrel distortion and dramatic foreshortening of [INSERT MODEL DESCRIPTION - e.g., a very attractive young fashion model (sharp jawline, symmetrical face, clean skin, confident expression)], styled like a [INSERT VIBE - e.g., luxury runway campaign].
[He/She/They] is wearing [INSERT OUTFIT DETAILS - e.g., a high-fashion GUCCI street-luxe outfit, premium layered fit, sleek black trousers], plus [INSERT ACCESSORIES - e.g., cool designer glasses].
Location: [INSERT LOCATION - e.g., Sweden (clean Scandinavian architecture, Stockholm street vibe, bright sky, crisp Nordic daylight)].
The model is [INSERT POSE - e.g., crouched / seated in a dynamic pose], leaning toward the camera with extreme wide-angle perspective and bulging barrel distortion, reaching out as if showcasing the item to the viewer. [He/She/They] is prominently displaying [INSERT MAIN OBJECT - e.g., a luxury perfume bottle] directly to the lens, with no other objects in hand.
Extreme perspective + 3D cut-out effect: The arm and hand holding the [INSERT MAIN OBJECT] are extremely close to the lens and feel huge due to the dramatic forced perspective. The hand and the [INSERT MAIN OBJECT] push far outside the photo into the extra-thick white border, creating a strong 3D border-break / cut-out poster effect. The [INSERT MAIN OBJECT] should appear to hang even further into the white border, with the [INSERT SECONDARY DETAIL] also slightly crossing into the border for extra depth. The effect should make it feel like the [INSERT MAIN OBJECT] is physically coming out of the poster.
Typography (minimal, luxury feel): Top-left: '[INSERT TEXT 1 - e.g., GUCCI]'. Top-right: small circular badge with '[INSERT TEXT 2 - e.g., 01]' inside. Right side vertical: '[INSERT TEXT 3 - e.g., NEW SEASON]'. Bottom: '[INSERT TEXT 4 - e.g., Worldwide since 1921]'."
Part 2: Animation Prompt Generation
After providing the image prompt, generate a customized Grok animation prompt. You must explicitly describe the specific motion and action clearly within the prompt, making sure to use the correct pronouns that match the character generated in Part 1. Do not use generic words like "moves"; instead, explicitly state what the character is doing with the specific object they are holding.
Baseline Animation Prompt Template:
"Commercial shot. The rectangle/poster border is locked in place and completely stationary. [INSERT CLEAR, SPECIFIC ACTION HERE — e.g., The model showcases the item, pushing the silver boombox...] outside the image of the rectangle into the surrounding white space, creating a 3D pop-out effect."
Tip: Emphasize that the border does NOT move. The magic of the effect is the contrast between the still frame and the moving model.
