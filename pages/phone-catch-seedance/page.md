Character consistency lock: Young woman, curly auburn hair, freckles, hazel eyes, wearing white blouse, dark green short skirt, and white sneakers.
SHOT-BY-SHOT EFFECTS TIMELINE
SHOT 1 (00:00–00:01) — Café Entry
EFFECT: Speed ramp (deceleration) + heavy motion blur
Camera enters mid-whip-pan from right, warm café light streaking across the frame
Decelerates rapidly from blur to a sharp image of a woman mid-lunge off a café stool
Dutch angle approximately 20° — the world is already tilting
A phone is visible sliding off the edge of a tall café table to her left
SHOT 2 (00:01–00:03) — The Freeze: Wide
EFFECT: Bullet-time orbit (counter-clockwise) + total time freeze
Camera begins smooth counter-clockwise orbit at table height around the woman
Woman completely frozen — body diagonal, horizontal lunge, right arm extended downward
Phone frozen inches from the floor, screen facing down, mid-tumble rotation
Background café crowd frozen mid-gasp — rigid like statues
SHOT 3 (00:03–00:05) — The Freeze: Phone ECU
EFFECT: Bullet-time orbit continues + digital zoom (scale-in) on phone
Camera continues orbit but punches in digitally toward the frozen phone
Screen facing down — a split second from shattering on hard café tile
Fine hairline light refracting off the glass screen edge, catching warm café light
This is the SIGNATURE VISUAL EFFECT — the phone suspended an inch from floor, screen-down, doom imminent
SHOT 4 (00:05–00:07) — The Freeze: Face ECU
EFFECT: Bullet-time orbit continues + slow push-in
Camera travels around to the woman's face — frozen in pure focused grimace
Eyes locked downward on the phone, jaw set, fingers splayed wide ready to catch
Orbit slows imperceptibly as camera approaches front-facing position
SHOT 5 (00:07–00:08) — Time Release
EFFECT: Speed ramp (acceleration from zero) + camera shake + zoom-out
Camera holds front-facing — woman's outstretched hand, phone, floor all aligned
Time releases: hand clamps around the phone with a single clean snap
Camera jolts with a single post-production shake on contact
Woman slowly rights herself, turns the phone over to check the screen
SHOT 6 (00:08–00:10) — Triumph
EFFECT: Slow-motion (approximately 40% speed) + warm light bloom
Crowd erupts in slow motion behind her — arms raised, mouths open
She turns the phone to face the crowd — screen fully intact, not a crack
Warm light bloom across the frame as she holds the phone aloft
Café stool still mid-topple behind her, frozen in its own slow collapse
Fade out on her grinning face, phone raised like a trophy
